User Research

The work of the alpha is shaped by the needs of our users both inside and outside of the Land Registry, which we discover by iterating the digital service and conversations which arise from experiments and prototypes. We have carried out Qualitative Research including Interviews, Contextual Inquiry and Usability testing. We have met citizens, volume conveyancers, small and medium conveyancing firms, lenders, estate agents and utility firms in over 60 meetings
